The mechanism

Simple. Honest.
Impossible to fake.

Built for the moments focus is hardest: meetings, lectures, long study sessions. The situations where your mind drifts and nobody notices. Especially you.

1

Start a focus session

Set your session length and alert frequency. Put your phone face-up or in your pocket.

2

Your phone vibrates. Randomly.

FocusPulse buzzes at unpredictable intervals. Your brain can't prepare for it. You can't game it.
